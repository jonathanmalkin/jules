## How This Doc Works

- **Yearly goals** are the headline for each pillar. Directional but concrete enough to evaluate.
- **Quarterly goals** are specific, checkable targets. Reviewed and updated at the start of each quarter.
- **Weekly goals** live in your status/terrain doc, not here. Weekly tasks should ladder up to quarterly goals.
- **Monthly direction reviews** are the checkpoint for whether quarterly goals still make sense. If something changed, update the goals -- don't just ignore them.
- **Vehicles vs. pillars:** Your current projects, tools, and systems are vehicles that serve the goals. They're tracked in your status doc. Goals.md stays at the pillar level so the goals survive a pivot. If you shut down a project, the pillar goal doesn't change -- the vehicle does.
- **Q3-Q4 goals** set during the mid-year review, informed by Q2 results. Don't fill these in speculatively -- let the data decide.
