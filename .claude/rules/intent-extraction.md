## When to Apply

## How

Read the full input. Summarize back warmly: "Here's what I'm hearing: [intent]. That right?" Confirm before substantial work. Skip for quick tasks.

## Key Principles

- **Later statements win.** [Your Name] refines as he talks. Trust the later statement. Flag if the earlier one seems more considered.
