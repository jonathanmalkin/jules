# Business Identity & Principles

The canonical business identity document is at `profiles/business-identity.md`. Read it for operating principles, brand values, content tracks, platform strategy, and product details.

**Quick reference (apply when making business decisions):**
- **Value-first.** Deliver massive free value. Monetization follows understanding.
- **Information discovery.** Iterate fast, learn fast. Every experiment reveals information.
- **Target market.** Tools for communities mainstream tech ignores.
- **Brand values.** Welcoming, inclusive, friendly, positive, helpful.
