# LinkedIn Reference

Algorithm mechanics, format specs, and engagement benchmarks for LinkedIn content. Loaded alongside `platform-templates.md` when writing LinkedIn posts.

Research source: `Documents/Field-Notes/Research/2026-03-30-LinkedIn-Effectiveness-AI-Era.md`

## Algorithm Mechanics (360Brew)

- **Expertise matching:** 360Brew (150B params) matches creator expertise to post topics. Write what you're known for — the algorithm finds the right audience. Writing outside your expertise graph confuses distribution.
- **Dwell time:** Practitioners who read longer = stronger signal. Long-form authority posts reward this.
- **Engagement weight:** Saves > Comments > Likes. Question closes drive comments. Valuable content drives saves.
- **Format penalty:** Same format 3x in a row = ~20% reach penalty. Rotate between text, carousels, images, video.
- **Link penalty:** URLs in body = ~60% reach reduction. Links go in first comment only.
- **Hashtag scanning:** Algorithm scans post copy for topic context. 3-5 specific hashtags (e.g., #ClaudeCode, #AIBuilder over #AI, #Tech). Specific > generic.
- **Golden hour:** First 60 minutes of engagement heavily weighted. Respond to every comment immediately.

## Format Performance (Engagement Rates)

| Format | Avg Engagement | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Document/PDF carousel | 6.6% | Highest. 1080x1350, 8-12 slides, upload as PDF |
| Polls | 5.8% | Use sparingly — feels gimmicky if overused |
| Image posts | 4.2% | Screenshots, diagrams, before/after |
| Video | 3.1% | Short (<90s). Captions required. |
| Text-only | 2.4% | Our previous default. Lowest reach. |

**Format rotation plan:** Never same format 3x in a row. Suggested cycle: Text → Carousel → Image → Text → Video → repeat. Adapt based on content fit.

## Length Sweet Spots (Bimodal)

| Type | Characters | Use Case |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Short** | 100-300 | Conversation starters, hot takes, questions. High engagement per character. |
| **Dead zone** | 300-1,000 | Too long to skim, too short for depth. Avoid. |
| **Long** | 1,300-1,900 | Authority posts, walkthroughs, stories. High dwell time. |

## Carousel Specs

- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1350 px (portrait — takes up more feed space)
- **Slide count:** 8-12 optimal
- **Upload format:** PDF for best rendering
- **Structure:** Hook slide → Context → Steps/Points (one per slide) → Takeaway → CTA slide
- **Design:** Clean, readable. Dark text on light background. One idea per slide. Large text (minimum 24pt equivalent).
- **Hand-drawn/lo-fi:** Works as occasional differentiator, not a primary strategy.

## Hook Formulas (REACT Framework)

First 140 characters must stop the scroll. LinkedIn truncates after ~140 chars on mobile with "...see more."

**R**esults: Lead with a specific outcome
**E**motion: Personal stake or feeling
**A**gitation: The problem that drove the action
**C**redibility: Why you're the one to talk about this
**T**ime: Urgency or timeliness

### Hook Examples by Story

**Story 1 (Systems/Infrastructure):**
- "I automated my entire content pipeline with Claude Code. 12 articles, 4 platforms, zero manual formatting." (R)
- "My AI assistant caught a bug I'd been shipping for 3 weeks." (E+R)

**Story 2 (Contrarian/Thesis):**
- "I build AI products for a market every VC told me to avoid." (A+C)
- "Kink education follows the exact same trajectory as meditation apps. Here's the data." (R+T)

**Story 3 (Applied Results):**
- "One person. No employees. 200+ daily users. Here's the stack." (R+C)
- "I replaced a 4-person content team with Claude Code and a bash script." (R+A)
